# ApplicantHelpBot
**ApplicantHelpBot** is a bot that helps applicants in finding HI(Higher Institution)

## Features:
- search for cities (1300 cities available !), HIs and programs
- get information about city, include location
- finding HIs in city
- get information about HI
- finding programs in HI
- get information about programs
- moving between city, HI and program


## Technologies:
- aiogram
- asyncio
- environs
- SQLAlchemy
